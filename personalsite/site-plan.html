Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to take us back to the 90's and early 2000's, and relive some of our most fond childhood memories. This will be a personal account of the toys and media I grew up with, and why I feel great nostalgia for them.

Audience

My audience will primarily be people born in the 90's.

Content

Home page

The aesthetic for the homepage will be themed by the early 2000’s. When I think about this time I think about bright colors, clear plastic electronics, and honestly a design that would be considered tacky by todays standards. In introducing what mattered to me and influenced me as a kid, I want to be true to the time. That being said I still want a level of cleanness to the presentation, because I want to make it easy to navigate from one page to the next. Fun, childlike, but still clean.

Images for the Home page

Games and Toys

For this section of the website I want to back even further and go for an 80’s pixel art design. I to this day am into retro gaming and retro hardware. There is something about sprites in video games that come off as very clean despite being a very old form of rendering a game. The color scheme for this part of the website will also be based upon the colors of the Nintendo 64 logo. Each game I talk about will have an accompanying picture, and I’ll try to organize them my time period.

Images for the Page 2

Movies and TV

When I was young, I loved Spider-man, SpongeBob SquarePants, The Fairly Odd Parents, The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, Full House and MythBusters. With this in mind I want to design this page to be both colorful, and with a similar style to those 90’s paper cups. (Picture below related.) When I talk about a show or movie, I will talk about a scene or episode that really stood out to me and I got excited for. There will be a related picture for each show or movie
